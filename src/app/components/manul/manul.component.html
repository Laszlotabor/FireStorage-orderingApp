You're probably wondering, *why is a wild cat staring at me from the background of a stock management app?*

Well, the truth is: the morning I started building this, I went to the zoo. I saw a Pallas's cat — this fluffy, grumpy little legend — and I just couldn’t stop thinking about it.

It looked like it didn’t care about anything, and somehow, that energy felt perfect for the project.

So no, there's no deep symbolism. It’s just a cool animal. I liked it. Now it's the spiritual mascot of this app.
