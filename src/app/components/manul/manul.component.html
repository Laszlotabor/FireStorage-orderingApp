You're probably wondering, "Why *Manul Storage*? What ancient logic compels someone to name a stock management app after a grumpy-looking wild feline from the steppes of Central Asia?"

Maybe you expect a profound metaphor — perhaps the Pallas’s cat symbolizes the quiet wisdom of inventory control? Or perhaps it's a stealthy predator of disorganized stockrooms?

No. It's none of that.

The truth is: I saw a picture of a manul, laughed for like 3 minutes, and thought "Yes. This is the face of enterprise-grade logistics."

So now, here we are. Managing shelves with the emotional support of a vaguely judgmental cat.

There is no deeper reason.

But it’s cute.

Good day.
